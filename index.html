Iron Maiden has gone down down as one of the best metal bands of all time. They weren't the first, that was Black Sabbath, they are one of the best Lets break it down, But, first you should see their website and listen to a song HERE

#1, Lyrical Content

When you listen to the radio what songs do you hear usually? Pop right? Then what are those pop songs about? LOVE OF COURSE! Love song after love song AFTER LOVE SONG! It gets to a point where every song is the same. Iron maiden takes this formula and throws it out the window in order to deliver there 100% own ideas to the table. a song about being stuck in time, a song about a man having powers because he is the seventh son of a seventh son. A song about war, ace combat, Deep intlectual philosophy, talking about the way he treated a mans daughter and how the evil that men do lives on and on! some songs are just instrumentals and they are insane! Losfer words (big orra) is one good Instrumental along with Transylvania.
